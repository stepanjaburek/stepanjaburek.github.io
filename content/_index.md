I am a first-year PhD student at the [Institute of Political Studies](https://ips.fsv.cuni.cz/en) at Charles University in Prague.

In my dissertation, I research elite political communication about international cooperation and information flows about international politics more broadly. With my co-authors, I also study the dimensionality of elite political ideology and its variation across European political contexts.

Methodologically I specialize in computational political science with a focus on Natural Language Processing and statistical modeling (slowly turning Bayesian).
