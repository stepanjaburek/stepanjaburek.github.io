I am a first-year PhD student at the [Institute of Political Studies](https://ips.fsv.cuni.cz/en) at Charles University in Prague, working with Michal Parizek, and a researcher within the Horizon Europe project ENSURED.

In my dissertation, I research how domestic political incentives shape elite communication and behavior towards global economic governance and international economic cooperation more broadly.

With co-authors, I also study how authoritarian legacies affect ideological debates — specifically, whether "left" and "right" carry (a)symmetric connotations across European political contexts.

Methodologically, I specialize in quantitative political science, with a focus on Natural Language Processing and statistical modeling (slowly turning Bayesian).
